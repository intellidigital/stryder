Understanding People

Companies don't understand their people or themselves. People don't understand their companies. We help companies and people understand themselves.

How we started it

Jeff Rooks realized while he was a recruiter that his job could be replaced. So he set out on a mission to replace himself with robots.

Why?

74% of the US workforce is open to a new job, but aren't actively searching. Only 30% of the US workforce is engaged at work. And recruiting firms have the highest turnover. If they can't staff for themselves, what makes you think they can staff for you?
