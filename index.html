ABOUT

Understanding People

Companies spend too much time and money analyzing resumes and details of candidates, without being able to build a complete picture of how that person will perform in a specific role.

Our Solution

Build a platform using artificial intelligence that allows decision makers to compare and contrast candidates by skill, culture fit, job stability, education quality, motive to move, and personality (in context to that specific company).

Why?

74% of the US workforce is open to a new job, but aren't actively searching. Only 30% of the US workforce is engaged at work. And recruiting firms have the highest turnover. If they can't staff for themselves, what makes you think they can staff for you?
